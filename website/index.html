About Us

A Legacy of Education Since 1952

Muhammadia Girls High School has been committed to providing affordable, quality education to underprivileged girls for over 70 years.

The Founders, Muhammadia Foundation

Established in 1952 by our visionary parents, Muhammadia Girls High School began as a humble tuition center and evolved into a beacon of hope for countless girls in Lahore. Our mission remains steadfast: to empower young girls through English medium education, ensuring they have the tools to build a brighter future, regardless of their financial background.
